Do we really need arch support?

For people with Low Or High arch having many problems related to pain and alignment in the body, Customised insoles offer proper alignment and balance. Runners with normal arch need arch support for harm prevention and shock absorption. human beings stricken by damage or pain in the lower extremities need it for ache control and restoration.

MYOELECTRIC PROSTHESIS

What is a Myoelectric prosthesis?

"Myoelectric” is that the term for electric blocks of muscles. A myoelectric- scaled prosthesis is an externally powered artificial limb that you just control with the electrical signals generated naturally by your ownmuscles.Hand, wrist and elbow myoelectric factors are available. With amputations above the elbow, a “ cross prosthesis” may combine myoelectric- controlled . elements with body-powered elements to manage shoulder and/ or elbow function. A skin-suchlike glove covers the prosthesis for a natural appearance.
